Our History

A Legacy of Excellence Since 2009

Rines Secondary School was founded in 2009 with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures the holistic development of every child. What started as a small community school has grown into one of the most respected secondary schools in the region.

Over the years, we have maintained our commitment to academic excellence while continuously evolving our teaching methodologies to meet the changing needs of our students. Our journey has been marked by numerous achievements and milestones that have shaped our identity.

Today, Rines Secondary School stands as a beacon of educational excellence, preparing students to become confident, creative, and compassionate leaders of tomorrow.
