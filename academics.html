New Lower Secondary Curriculum

Senior 1-4 (O-Level)

Competence-Based Approach

Focuses on practical skills, research, and project-based learning rather than rote memorization.

Reduced Subjects (12 total)

11 compulsory: English, Mathematics, History, Geography, Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Physical Education, Religious Education, Entrepreneurship, Kiswahili.

Continuous Assessment (CA)

20% of final UNEB marks from ongoing teacher assessments throughout 4-year cycle.

New Grading: A-E Levels

Competency-based reports (A, B, C, D, E) instead of percentage scores.

Thematic Integration

Holistic education combining Performing Arts (dance, music, drama) and integrated learning.

Learner-Centered

Active learning, group work, real-world projects preparing students for 21st century challenges.
