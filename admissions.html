What You Need

Requirements

New Students

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original & copy)
  • Recent passport photos (4 copies)
  • Transfer letter (if from another school)
  • Medical form from approved health facility
  • Parent/guardian ID copies
  • Previous school reports (if applicable)

Required Documents

  • Application form (online or physical)
